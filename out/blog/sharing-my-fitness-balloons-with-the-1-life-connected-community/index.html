Sharing My Fitness Balloon with the 1 Life Connected Community!

1 Life Connected, a wellness movement founded by Dr. Kimberly Pope-Robinson that supports fellow veterinarians, describes the balance of “balloons” and “sinkers” in our daily lives. Balloons lift us up and sinkers drag us down. In this video, I share with the 1 Life Connected Community how fitness acts as my balloon!
