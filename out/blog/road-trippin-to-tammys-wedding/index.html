Road Trippin' to Tammy's Wedding

I try to find any excuse to travel. When my awesome college friend, Tammy, invited me to her wedding in Santa Cruz, I looked for ways to maximize my trip.

I planned for a one-week trip with Mojo. I started from Los Angeles where I house-sat for my parents. My first stop was my best friend's house in San Jose. We spent a good amount of time of catching up and enjoying delicious meals. He had just started remodeling his new house, so I was excited to be there to share that process with him.

The wedding was absolutely beautiful. The cherry on top was the boba bar â€“ simply the best wedding idea, ever. I had two jasmine milk teas and took one to go.

We drove back to San Jose to catch up with a few more friends and played some golf, then headed back down to our sweet home in San Diego.

I enjoy traveling for many reasons. One of those is the chance to reconnect with my friends. They remind me of the things I cherish and appreciate the most about life!
